TranceBox Media Player is a Media Player with the basic functions to start, pause, stop or mute an audio file.
It embeds with it a frequence Wave visualizer. Along with a trackbar to track its progress.
The trackbar for volume controls the value of volume while that of balance controls the amplitude of volume from both the speakers.
Also there are controls for bass and treble.
The Media Player also shows the information of the audio file tagged with it. Thus fulfilling all the basic needs of a media player.
